# Amanda Natividad — Zero-Click Content Era: AI Overviews Impact on Search Behavior (LinkedIn, ~2026-03-05)
Source: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_nobodys-clicking-anymore-and-its-not-activity-7414697334942576640-p_NS

Nobody’s clicking anymore—and not because they bounce. They finish the journey inside AI Overviews.
- Pew (Mar 2025): when an AI Overview appears, users click a traditional result 8% of the time vs 15% without it; sessions end right there 26% vs 16%.
- Seer Interactive (Sep 2025): organic CTR on queries with AI Overviews dropped to 0.61% from 1.76% in Jun 2024 (‑61%).

What users do instead:
• Read the AI Overview and stop
• Search again without leaving Google
• Default to Wikipedia/YouTube/Reddit
• Ask an LLM chatbot
• Or just keep scrolling social

Amanda’s takeaway: Zero-Click Content is now the default reality. Optimize to be the cited, remembered source in AI answers and feeds—clicks are a bonus, not the plan.
