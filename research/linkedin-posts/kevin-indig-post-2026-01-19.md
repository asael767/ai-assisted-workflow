# Kevin Indig — Moving Beyond Old SEO Models in the Age of AI (2026-01-19)
Source: https://www.advancedwebranking.com/blog/beyond-old-seo-models-ai-search

This long-form interview captures Kevin Indig’s playbook-level guidance for AI-era SEO. Core insights:
- Shift from keywords to prompts and entities: build content so AI can retrieve, synthesize, and cite you across conversational fan-out.
- Proprietary data as moat: original datasets and benchmarks are the strongest differentiator for AI Overviews and chat answers.
- Measure influence, not just traffic: track citation share in AI answers and branded recall triggered by AI output; rankings alone are decoupled from conversions.
- UX for zero-click reality: when AI Overviews do send traffic, pages must surface answers instantly and guide visitors to conversion paths.
- Small teams can win with AI-assisted velocity plus clear information architecture and structured data.
- Unlearn strict keyword mapping; optimize for being the most credible source the model selects.

The article includes Kevin’s detailed explanations, examples of AI answer patterns, and recommendations on PR, schema, and content depth to become the default citation.
