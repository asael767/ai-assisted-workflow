# Jeff Coyle — Content Marketing Trends for 2026 (LinkedIn, 2026-02-02)
Source: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jeffcoyle_42-experts-reveal-top-content-marketing-trends-activity-7404146944497012737-wE80

Jeff responds to Content Marketing Institute’s 2026 trends piece, highlighting what he believes actually moves the needle:
- Hybrid retrieval becomes the differentiator: combining vector + symbolic search so content is discoverable via AI Overviews and traditional search in parallel.
- Funnels need fixing more than “more content”: he urges teams to repair conversion paths and tie experiments to revenue, not vanity traffic.
- Benchmark with peers who publish proof: he tags practitioners (A. Lee Judge, Andy Crestodina, David Fortino, Brian Piper, Robert Rose) to emphasize outcomes over opinions.
- Invite public scrutiny: asks readers to share their 2026 prediction and commits to replying, modeling open signal-sharing instead of generic “top trends” listicles.

Use this as a playbook anchor for CMI-style executive presentations: show how hybrid retrieval and funnel repair turn AI visibility into pipeline, not just impressions.
