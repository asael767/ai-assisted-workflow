I'm bullish on the quality of SEO chatter in 2026 because:

1) More practitioners share experiments and results. Less guru fluff.
2) AI forces SEOs to level up strategic thinking.
3) Smaller teams are publishing 10x faster with AI-assisted workflows.
4) Execs finally ask about "AI visibility" not just "rankings".
5) We're starting to measure influence, not just clicks.

It took a messy 2025 to get here, but the signal-to-noise ratio is improving. I'm optimistic for the craft.
