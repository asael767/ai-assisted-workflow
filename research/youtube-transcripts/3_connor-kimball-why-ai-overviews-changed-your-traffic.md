Long-form transcript excerpt
- Connor: “AI Overviews collapsed our client’s homepage clicks by 38% overnight, but branded search and direct rose 22%. The win comes from being cited, not being clicked.”

- On content format: “We rebuild top pages into entity-dense FAQs and comparison tables so LLMs can lift clean chunks. Schema everywhere: product, how-to, FAQ, and organization.”
- On link strategy: “Digital PR still moves the needle, but anchor needs to reinforce entities. We avoid random ‘AI humanizer’ fluff that breaks consistency.”
- Playbook steps he outlines:
  1) Map prompts → entities → pages; instrument schema.
  2) Publish 3 proprietary data pieces per quarter to earn citations.
  3) Track answer share weekly across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Grok.
  4) Tie AI citation lifts to pipeline via direct + branded attribution.
