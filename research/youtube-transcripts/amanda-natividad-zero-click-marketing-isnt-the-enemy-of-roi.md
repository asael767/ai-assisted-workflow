# Zero-Click Marketing Isn’t the Enemy of ROI — It’s the Future (Move the Needle podcast, Ep. 170) — Amanda Natividad
Source: https://pca.st/episode/ee70a1f8-9765-4c61-809b-999d3e421b13

Long-form transcript excerpt
- Amanda: Zero-click content isn’t about starving traffic; it’s about earning trust where people already are. “If you only optimize for clicks, you only reach the people already ready to buy. Zero-click earns attention earlier and keeps you top of mind when they do search by brand.”
- On metrics: “Track saves, replies, and re-shares as leading indicators. They show the content changed someone’s mind. Clicks lag; saves lead.”
- On repurposing: “Every long-form asset should produce a carousel, a thread, a short, and an email. Distribution is productization.”
- On AI Overviews: “Your brand’s best moat is proprietary examples and proof. AI can paraphrase, but it can’t invent your case study.”
- On ROI proof: Amanda walks through SparkToro’s funnel where zero-click posts lift branded search and direct traffic; those convert 2–3x higher than cold clicks.
- On team process: “We run a weekly ‘evidence review’ where we collect screenshots, comments, and demo requests that cite the zero-click posts—that’s how we justify budget.”

Why this is high-signal: full-funnel framing, concrete metrics, and operational cadence you can lift directly into a playbook.
