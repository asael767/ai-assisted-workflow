Long-form transcript excerpt (high-signal sections)
- Goal: win the cited answer in ChatGPT/Perplexity for a 7-figure ACV B2B SaaS across 18 high-intent queries.
- Playbook outlined:
  1) Query fan-out mapping → entity graph; aligned page clusters to intents instead of single keywords.
  2) Content rebuild: FAQ + comparison tables + pricing clarity; each page shipped with Product/FAQ/HowTo schema and llms.txt.
  3) Proprietary data drop: published cohort benchmarks and uptime stats; used as digital PR hook to earn 12 authority links.
  4) Reinforcement loop: weekly LLM visibility checks; prompts tuned for ICP language to avoid “marketing-speak” bias.
- Results after 60 days:
  • ChatGPT/Perplexity top-citation rate: 64% of tracked prompts (up from 11%).
  • Organic clicks +41% (though clicks were secondary), direct + branded sessions +27% credited to AI answer recall.
  • Sales: +19% SQLs from branded/direct; 3 deals cited the comparison table surfaced inside AI answers.
- Process notes: separate KPIs for AEO (answer share, citation rate, entity coverage) vs SEO (traffic, position); do not conflate.
