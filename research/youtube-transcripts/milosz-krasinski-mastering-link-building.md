# Mastering Link Building & SEO Strategies with Milosz Krasiński (Clicks to Customers, May 2025)

Host John Horn (StubGroup) interviews Milosz Krasiński, founder of Chili Fruit, on why link building remains a long‑term, compounding asset and how to pair it with content, digital PR, and PPC data to drive conversions. Krasiński stresses that backlinks are “digital assets” that appreciate when acquired consistently, likening the process to building a six‑pack: you cannot sprint your way to durable results. He cautions against chasing DR80+ lists for vanity; one relevant trade‑site link often beats ten generic directory placements. Fresh content plus trustworthy backlinks still signal relevance to search engines and even influence how LLMs surface brands. Press releases alone rarely move rankings—he layers targeted anchors and internal link updates after earned coverage. PPC keyword data reveals converting intents that shape linkable assets and outreach angles, keeping SEO tied to revenue.
